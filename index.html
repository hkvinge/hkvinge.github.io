About me:

I am a doctoral researcher at the UC Davis Department of Mathematics. My research focuses on representation theory (which is roughly the mathematical study of symmetry) and algebraic combinatorics.

I will graduate in June. In July I will begin a 2-year postdoc in the Pattern Analysis Lab at Colorado State University. I will be working on projects:

  • investigating compressive sensing algorithms for hyperspectral and LIDAR imaging applications as part of a university-industry team.

  • studying how to use machine learning algorithms to efficiently detect biomarkers.
