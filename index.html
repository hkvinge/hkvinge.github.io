About me:

I am a mathematician currently employed as a postdoctoral fellow in the Pattern Analysis Lab at Colorado State University. I work on problems in both pure and applied mathematics. Some of my research interests are listed below.

Representation theory, categorification, and combinatorics:
  • Heisenberg categories,
  • quiver Hecke algebras (KLR algebras),
  • symmetric groups and related Hecke algebras,
  • symmetric functions,
  • quantum group crystals.
Applied math and data science
  • Machine learning (particularly neural networks),
  • compressive sensing,
  • geometric methods in data science,

I received my PhD in Mathematics from UC Davis in June 2017, my advisor was Monica Vazirani.
